FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Quantrex App really free? +

Yes! The core Quantrex App — NCERT Qs Bank, DPP, Formula Cards & more — is 100% free forever. You only pay if you choose optional Quantrex Premium for advanced features.

Which exams does Quantrex cover? +

Quantrex covers JEE Main, JEE Advanced, NEET UG, Foundation (Class 9-10) & Board Exams. Choose Engineering, Medical or Foundation when you sign up.

Can I use Quantrex on the web? +

Yes! Quantrex is available on Android, iOS & now on the web at quantrexacademy.vercel.app. Your progress syncs across all devices.

How does the leaderboard & leagues work? +

Every correct answer earns points. Climb the weekly & All India leaderboards, get promoted through leagues (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Legend) & win exciting rewards.

Do you provide solutions for every question? +

Absolutely. Every question comes with detailed step-by-step written solutions & many have video solutions too for complete clarity.

Can I create my own custom tests? +

Yes! Build custom tests by selecting subjects, chapters, difficulty & number of questions — perfect for targeted practice.

How do I add or verify my mobile number? +

You need to add & verify your mobile number to continue using your Quantrex account. Go to Profile → Add Mobile Number, enter your number, verify with OTP & you're set.

Who makes the Quantrex App? +

Quantrex is built by Quantrex Academy, India's leading ed-tech company focused on affordable, high-quality JEE & NEET preparation. Reach us anytime at support@quantrexacademy.com.
